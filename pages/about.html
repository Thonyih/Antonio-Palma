Early Life

I was born in Portugal, on the 8th of June of 1999, I grew up in the suburbs of Lisbon, I spent my early youth trying many hobbies, playing computer games, hanging out with family, studying in school and meeting new friends

Teenager

During my teenage years, I fell in love with my main hobby, mountain biking, and made many new friends along the way. Most of my time was spent riding, fixing, and exploring new places to ride my bicycle. Additionally, I started my first job, trained almost every day, got my driving and motorcycle license, and finished high school.

Young Adult

As a young adult, I began studying Computer Engineering at university. My love for computers and bicycles kept growing, and I also fell in love with motorsports, including cars and motorcycles. I started fixing and working on my car, eventually taking it to a racetrack. I also fixed my own motorcycle to keep it running on a low budget. I began working as a Junior Developer, gaining my first hands-on experience in the IT field. Additionally, I traveled around Europe, gained new experiences and made new friends and colleagues.

At the Moment

I made some big steps in my life, finishing my Computer Engineering degree at university with a good average. I embarked on a new professional adventure, working on a European project, helping to develop software interfaces for innovative medical equipment. I also challenged myself personally and started competing in mountain bike races. My goal is to maintain this momentum into the future, constantly looking for the next professional and personal challenge.
